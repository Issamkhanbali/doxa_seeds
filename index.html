About Doxa Seeds

Doxa Seeds is a global wholesale supplier of premium vegetable seeds, dedicated to the development and distribution of innovative, high-quality varieties. Headquartered in Holland, Doxa Seeds collaborates closely with leading breeders and researchers worldwide to create cutting-edge solutions tailored to the evolving needs of the market. Supported by a robust trialing and distribution network, Doxa Seeds is committed to delivering the right varieties—precisely when and where you need them.

DOXA Seeds: Setting the Standard in Solanaceae Excellence
With a strong legacy in the Solanaceae segment, DOXA Seeds has built a reputation for excellence in a highly specialized and competitive market.
Our elongated tomato varieties have become the standard in Italy, Central America, and key markets across the Middle East. At the same time, our sweet peppers are rapidly gaining ground in European export markets, thanks to their outstanding quality, uniformity, and shelf life.
If you’ve tasted hot pepper sauce, paste, or powder in parts of North Africa, there’s a good chance you’ve already experienced the flavor of a DOXA hot pepper—our genetics are the go-to choice for processors and growers in this region.
Completing our portfolio is a powerful rootstock range, trusted for:

  • Strong root development,
  • Reliable disease resistance, and
  • A broad selection tailored to every vigor requirement in diverse growing conditions.

At DOXA Seeds, we don’t just follow market trends—we define them.

Vegetable Seeds

Doxa Seeds is a wholesale vegetable seed company based in California. Doxa Seeds partners with breeders from around the world to develop innovative new hybrid and open pollinated vegetable varieties. Breeding stations, production fields, and trialing locations are placed strategically around the world, ensuring the optimal genetics, highest quality seed, and best research available. Doxa Seeds specializes in developing products for tropical climates.

Products

Doxa Seeds offers a full product line of hybrid, improved open pollinated, and generic open pollinated products for the open field, net house, and greenhouse environments. The primary market focus is on hybrid tomatoes, peppers, brassicas, onions, carrot, cucumber, and sweet corn. Doxa Seeds also holds significant market share in bean, melon, watermelon, cilantro, lettuce, baby corn, beet, pea, radish, papaya, broccoli, and other products. Please visit the Products page to view more about varieties specifically selected for your growing conditions.

Trials

Doxa Seeds staff are industry leaders in the selection of next generation hybrid varieties. With hundreds of years of combined product development experience, the Doxa Seeds team travels the world to numerous trialing locations in dozens of countries, sorting through thousands of varieties to select the highest performing products. These products are selected for both local adaptability, as well as superior performance in an array of growing conditions, ensuring that the products will perform well for growers year after year.
